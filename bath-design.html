Bathroom Design

The bathroom is your comfort zone, your oasis, and your place to relax, and escape the day.

American Carpet's design professionals will help you make your dream bathroom a reality. A new bathroom can be starting point of your home remodel, or the final piece that completes your home lifestyle. With our endless selection of products such as sinks, vanities, fixtures, and more, we will assist you in planning a bathroom design that you will love.

We Can Help

Our team will guide you through the process of making the choices necessary to complete the project. If you haven't already begun research on bathroom remodeling you will be amazed at the choices you have. Whether you are looking at granite or marble countertops, single or double sinks, a whirlpool tub or soft tub, or ceramic wall tiling, we will work closely with you so that the final project reflects your personal taste and sets just the right tone.
